About Us

Founded in 2006, Absolute Adventure Safaris is a premier transport and travel company based in Kenya. We specialize in taxi services, air ticketing, car hire, hotel reservations, and safari packages. Our goal is to exceed expectations through innovative, customer-focused travel solutions.

Mission

To grow and transform the transport industry through innovative solutions that exceed our customers' expectations.

Vision

To provide high-quality 24-hour transport services in Nairobi and its environs, prioritizing comfort, safety, and quick response.

Core Values

  • Upholding safety as our top priority
  • Understanding and tailoring to customer needs
  • Hiring and rewarding top-caliber employees
